The Ford Mustang is a legendary American sports car and muscle car that was first introduced
in 1964. It quickly became popular due to its sleek design, powerful engine, and affordability.
The Mustang has gone through several generations of redesigns and updates, but it has always
maintained its reputation as an iconic American muscle car.

The Ford Mustang Shelby is a high-performance version of the Ford Mustang produced
by the Ford Motor Company. t is named after Carroll Shelby, who was instrumental
in designing and engineering the original Mustang in the mid-1960s.
The Shelby Mustangs are known for their powerful engines,
aggressive styling, and race-inspired handling.

The latest iteration of the Shelby Mustang is the Shelby GT500, which boasts
a supercharged V8 engine that produces over 700 horsepower.
